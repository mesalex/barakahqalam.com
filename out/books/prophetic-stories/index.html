About This Book

Bring the timeless stories of the Prophets to life through art and reflection. This beautifully illustrated book includes: • 25+ stories from the lives of the Prophets (peace be upon them) • Full-page illustrations ready for coloring • Summarized stories in simple, accessible language • Quranic references for each story • Discussion questions for family learning • Arabic vocabulary words and their meanings Stories include: • Prophet Nuh (Noah) and the Ark • Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham) and his unwavering faith • Prophet Musa (Moses) and the parting of the sea • Prophet Yunus (Jonah) and the whale • Prophet Isa (Jesus) and his miracles • Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — the Seal of the Prophets Designed for: • Islamic weekend schools and madrasas • Family story time and discussions • Children ages 6-14 (and adults too!) • Ramadan evening activities • Building a love for the Prophets in young hearts
